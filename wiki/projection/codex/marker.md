# Marker

The loaded-skill marker is the Codex-visible projection of a handle.

Partita markers belong to active skills or skill families, not to
`harness/skills/dispatcher.md`.

`🧭` is the orientation-family marker. `🎼` is the Partita-managed base-layer
marker. `🧹` is the maintenance-family marker.

A marker must appear only when its owning skill is active.

See [[skill/lifecycle/marker]].
