# Marker

A marker is the runtime projection of a handle.

Markers may be shared by a skill family when the active skills share a
user-visible governance expectation.

The dispatcher does not own a marker. Active skills project their own marker:
`🧭` for the orientation family, `🎼` for the Partita-managed base layer, and
`🧹` for the maintenance family.

See [[projection/codex/marker]].
